countdown

A countdown website is a dynamic platform featuring a timer to build anticipation or urgency for an upcoming event, launch, or milestone.

Digital clock

A digital clock website displays the current time in a clear, modern format, often with additional features like date, time zone settings, and customizable styles for convenience and aesthetic appeal.

Radius

A radius website visually demonstrates the concept of a radius in geometry, typically featuring interactive elements or tools to calculate and explore the radius of circles and its applications in real-life contexts.


Password Generation

A password generation website is a tool that creates strong, secure, and random passwords based on user-defined criteria like length, inclusion of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers often with options to copy to the clipboard for easy use
